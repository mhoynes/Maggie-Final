About J. Bar...

Slightly hidden away off of the main strip through the center of beautiful Harwich Port, J. BAR is Cape Cod’s coolest & healthiest vegan cafe & juice bar. Serving you whole food – plant based foods which are basically not processed at all. Everything from our centrifugally-produced juices, creative & satisfying smoothies and wholesome fresh foods are simple & pure, – made to order always using the finest raw and living superfoods. It’s the kind of food a body needs to feel healthy, alive, energized and vibrant – while it also tastes fantastic!

For the health conscious person with special dietary needs such as Paleo, Vegan, Wheat Free, Gluten Free, Dairy Free, Raw Food or one just looking to empower themselves by eating healthier, YOU will be very happy with a visit to J. Bar.

J. Bar starts with the finest, freshest pesticide-free ingredients available. We then prepare each item using methods which ensure that their life-enhancing nutrients remain intact, delivering a higher form of energy directly to a body’s cells with each sip and every bite. All of our foods, juices, smoothies, acai bowls, supplements, iced cold detox drinks, salads, sandwiches, house made treats, snacks & salad dressing – even our sweet stuff – is pure, full of flavor & nutrition. What it’s not filled with is processed sugar, gluten & dairy products.

Owner Erin Tucker has channeled her decades of experience in pursuing a healthful way of life, and her natural creativity, into conceiving J. Bar’s menu and its art-filled, eclectic atmosphere. A lifelong lover of nature, the ocean and adventures, Erin grew up believing in the power of nature and has always been drawn to healthful eating. Preparing food with only natural ingredients, using techniques which allow our food to remain in its purest, most-beneficial form, is her passion (or one of them anyhow).
